About Maya AI

Increase Human Productivity
With Responsible AI

Company Bio

The Mayans at Maya AI are a group of passionate leaders, dedicated to blending the tactical prowess of RPA with the cognitive depot of AI, we enable businesses to automate complex processes, ensuring precision, efficiency, and ethical integration of cutting-edge technology into the human workflow.

Founder's Story

The three Ramphal brothers have been building responsible AI for the past decade in different corporations and startups.

The three of us have faced similar issues in implementing AI services into a workflow or process, it always seems to create inefficiency by adding more to a professionals work than seamlessly being integrated into a workflow. This is what we set out to solve.

 
THATS WHEN MAYA AI WAS BORN.

Mission

To inspire humanity to become more efficient in daily life by creating personal accurate knowledge. By developing innovative solutions that improve outcomes, drive operational efficiency, and support providers and organizations in delivering high-quality, cost-effective decisions.
